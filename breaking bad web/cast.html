Breaking Bad Cast

Bryan Cranston

Bryan Cranston plays Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer. Diagnosed with terminal lung cancer, Walter transforms from a mild-mannered family man to a ruthless drug kingpin, adopting the alias "Heisenberg."

Aaron Paul

Aaron Paul portrays Jesse Pinkman, a former student of Walter's and a small-time meth manufacturer. Jesse becomes Walt's partner in the meth business. Despite his criminal lifestyle, Jesse is sensitive and struggles with guilt and addiction throughout the series.

Anna Gunn

Anna Gunn plays Skyler White, Walter's wife, who initially is unaware of his illegal activities. Skyler becomes increasingly suspicious and eventually learns the truth, getting reluctantly involved in laundering Walt's drug money.

Dean Norris

Dean Norris portrays Hank Schrader, Walter's brother-in-law and a dedicated DEA agent. Hank is determined and often the comic relief, but his investigation into Albuquerque's meth scene ultimately brings him dangerously close to discovering Walt's secret.

Betsy Brandt

Betsy Brandt plays Marie Schrader, Skyler’s sister and Hank’s wife. Marie is often seen dealing with her kleptomania and supporting Hank through his struggles.

Bob Odenkirk

Bob Odenkirk portrays Saul Goodman, a flamboyant and morally flexible lawyer who becomes the legal counsel for Walt and Jesse. Saul's expertise in navigating the criminal underworld proves invaluable.

Giancarlo Esposito

Giancarlo Esposito plays Gus Fring, a highly intelligent and composed drug lord who operates under the guise of a legitimate businessman with his chain of Los Pollos Hermanos restaurants.

Jonathan Banks

Jonathan Banks portrays Mike Ehrmantraut, a former police officer who works for Gus and later for Walt and Jesse as a private investigator, enforcer, and cleaner. Mike is highly skilled, professional, and deeply cares for his granddaughter.
