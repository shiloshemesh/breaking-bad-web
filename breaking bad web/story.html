Season 1

Summary: High school chemistry teacher Walter White is diagnosed with terminal lung cancer. To secure his family's financial future, he partners with former student Jesse Pinkman to cook and sell methamphetamine. Their first attempts are fraught with danger and mistakes, including encounters with local drug dealers Krazy-8 and Emilio, leading to violence. Walter's brother-in-law, Hank, a DEA agent, starts investigating the new meth operation in Albuquerque, unaware of Walt's involvement.

Season 2

Summary: Walt and Jesse's meth business grows, but so do the complications. They become entangled with psychotic distributor Tuco Salamanca. Jane Margolis, Jesse's landlord and girlfriend, introduces him to heroin. Skyler, Walt's wife, becomes suspicious of his behavior. The season culminates in a series of tragic events: Jane overdoses and dies, Jesse spirals into despair, and a mid-air collision occurs due to Jane's father's grief-stricken mistake as an air traffic controller.

Season 3

Summary: Walt attempts to distance himself from the drug trade, but the allure and pressure draw him back in. He works with Gus Fring, a meticulous drug lord, who offers him a state-of-the-art lab and a professional partner, Gale. Jesse struggles with guilt over Jane's death and begins a relationship with Andrea, who has a young son. Hank inches closer to discovering Walt's secret, leading to an intense and violent confrontation with Tuco's cousins. The season ends with Walt orchestrating Gale's murder to protect himself from being replaced.

Season 4

Summary: Walt and Jesse's partnership with Gus becomes increasingly strained. Gus plans to kill Walt and groom Jesse as his successor, leading Walt to plot against Gus. Jesse gets deeply involved with Andrea and her son, Brock, whose poisoning is later revealed as part of Walt's manipulative strategy. Hank continues his investigation, closing in on Gus. The season climaxes with a dramatic showdown where Walt and Jesse succeed in killing Gus with a bomb, freeing them from his control.

Season 5(part 1)

Summary: Walt, Jesse, and Mike start their own meth operation after Gus's death. They face challenges securing supplies and distributing their product. Lydia Rodarte-Quayle, a corporate executive, becomes a crucial ally. Walt's greed and ruthlessness grow, leading to conflicts with Mike and eventually Mike's death. Hank begins to piece together clues leading to Walt. The first part ends with Hank's shocking realization that Walt is Heisenberg.

Season 5(part 2)

Summary: Hank's discovery sets off a cat-and-mouse game between him and Walt. Jesse learns of Walt's betrayal and teams up with Hank. Their plan falls apart, leading to Hank's death at the hands of Jack Welker's gang, who then take most of Walt's money. Walt goes into hiding, but returns to Albuquerque to settle scores. In a final confrontation, he rescues Jesse from captivity, kills the gang, and succumbs to his injuries in the meth lab, ending his tumultuous journey.
